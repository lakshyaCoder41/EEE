About Us

DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

  • The department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering is one of the major departments being created in 2022 to achieve the mission and vision of the institute. The Department is currently in its development phase and engaged in teaching and research in the areas of power system and control, VLSI, Wireless communication, signal processing, and antennas.

  • At present the department offers M.Tech in VLSI and PhD programs in different areas such as VLSI, Signal processing, wireless communication, and power system and control. The Faculty members of the department are also engaged in outreach activities by organizing workshops, conferences and academic collaboration. Further, many Government sponsored research projects are also being undertaken by the faculty members of the department. The Vision of the Department is to achieve Global excellence through Electrical and Electronics Engineering education in knowledge Economy.

    • MISSION

  • Teaching, research and extension education in alignment with the new-age technologies and solutions.
  • Design and Development of integrative engineering and technology solutions with cross functional perspectives.
  • Nurturing, training and skilling the young minds for innovative engineering applications and solutions, and entrepreneurial mindset.
